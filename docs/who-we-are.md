# Who We Are

## The Vision

Dexetera was created to solve a problem: **centralized exchanges control too much**. They decide what you can trade, freeze accounts, require personal information, and profit from your trades.

We built Dexetera on a simple principle: **You should have complete control over your money and trades.**

## What We Believe

### Decentralization Works
Traditional finance requires intermediaries. Blockchain removes the need for them. Smart contracts execute trades fairly, transparently, and without bias.

### Privacy Matters
You shouldn't need to give away your identity to trade. Your financial activity is personal.

### Innovation Needs Freedom
The best markets emerge when anyone can create what they want. No gatekeepers. No limits.

### Transparency Builds Trust
Every transaction is on the blockchain. Nothing is hidden. You can verify everything.

## The Team

Dexetera is built by experienced developers with deep expertise in:

- **Blockchain & Smart Contracts**: Years building on Ethereum, Arbitrum, and other chains
- **Decentralized Finance (DeFi)**: Understanding trading, protocols, and risk management
- **Web Development**: Building fast, intuitive user interfaces
- **Security**: Smart contract audits, testing, and risk assessment

Our team spans multiple time zones and works around the clock to maintain and improve the platform.

## Our Commitment

### Security First
We regularly audit smart contracts, test extensively, and follow blockchain best practices.

### User Control
You always control your private keys. Dexetera never holds your funds—smart contracts do.

### Continuous Improvement
We listen to user feedback and update the platform based on real needs.

### Community Focused
We're not a corporation maximizing profits. We're a team building the future of trading.

## How We Make Money

Unlike centralized exchanges that profit from trading commissions, Dexetera:

- Charges **transparent trading fees** (visible before trading)
- Fees fund development and infrastructure
- No hidden revenues or proprietary trading

We don't:
- Sell user data
- Trade against you
- Use predatory features like forced liquidations
- Charge hidden fees
